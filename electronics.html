Electronics 101

Do you want to start playing with electronics but don't have any previous knowledge and everything seems too complicated?

Did you ever want to make a simple project with electronics but instructions weren't clear enough and you don't know where to ask for help?

This is the right place for you! Come and learn the basics of electronics through fun and easy maker projects. We will be soldering basic components that are used in many DIY projects and have such a variety of functions that you can make many different thingies by slightly modifying what you already made. Each term in this workshop is dedicated to making one project, so it's not necessary to come every time, but it is definitely recommended to participate in every project as some of them can be combined to make a different device in the end.
